The Best Bacon Sandwich

Bacon sandwiches are the perfect cure-all food. A mix of carbohydrate, fat and protein; this tasty on-the-go meal is sure to satisfy even the most intense hunger pangs.

Ingredients

  • Seeded bread
  • Bacon
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Fry the bacon until crispy 🥓
  2. Mash avocado 🥑 in a bowl with:
    • Salt 🧂
    • Lemon juice 🍋
    • Olive oil 🪔
  3. Slice tomato 🍅
  4. Add crispy bacon, lettuce, tomato and avocado to the seeded bread.🥪
  5. Enjoy!😋
